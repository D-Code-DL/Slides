What Is This Project?

D-Code is an after-school program in which students learn the fundamentals of coding & software development.

The program is held at Detroit Edison Public School Academy (DEPSA) Early College of Excellence, a high school just north of Eastern Market.

We meet twice a week.

Why Did We Decide to Do This?

We were already interested in community outreach.

We had been visiting city and metro area schools to speak with groups of students about what we did as app developers.

We saw a need.

-students asked if DL had internships, answer was no -we could only point them to resources and hope they figured it out -knew that their peers from wealthier backgrounds could access people, AP courses, clubs, etc

We were familiar with teaching.

-former math teacher -current mother, former education student
